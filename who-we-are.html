Who We Are

Your strategic growth partner, dedicated to transforming organizations through PMO excellence and innovative solutions.

From Strategy to Execution

At SpringboardPro, we serve as your strategic growth partner in execution excellence — a launchpad for success that helps organizations move with clarity and confidence from strategy to execution.

Much like a springboard, we provide the momentum to transform complex challenges into opportunities, paving the way for innovation, efficiency, and long-term value.

What defines us is not just what we do, but how we do it:

  • Structure & Discipline — ensuring every initiative stays on track.
  • Governance & Integrity — embedding controls and transparency.
  • Collaboration & Innovation — working with clients to deliver sustainable growth.

Backed by two decades of leadership and 3,000+ projects delivered, we bring together project management expertise, intelligent automation, and business insights.

"Strategies, though strong, often stumbled in execution."

After two decades in leadership roles at Oracle, HSBC, DHL, and Kinara Capital—where I drove business transformation, managed large-scale operations, and led 3,000+ projects — I identified a common challenge.

I founded SpringboardPro to bridge that gap. My vision is to enable organizations to confidently transition from strategy to execution, powered by governance, automation, and data-driven insights.

For me, SpringboardPro is more than a consulting firm; it is a launchpad for sustainable growth, collaboration, and success.

Cecilia D'Silva

Founder & CEO

Founder's Story

“Strategies, though strong, often stumbled in execution — so we built a partner that shepherds both ambition and delivery.”

After two decades in leadership roles at Oracle, HSBC, DHL, and Kinara Capital—where I drove business transformation, managed large-scale operations, and led 3,000+ projects — I identified a common challenge.

I founded SpringboardPro to bridge that gap. My vision is to enable organizations to confidently transition from strategy to execution, powered by governance, automation, and data-driven insights.

For me, SpringboardPro is more than a consulting firm; it is a launchpad for sustainable growth, collaboration, and success.
